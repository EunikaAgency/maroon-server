The 90 Degree Channel Galvanised Steel Corner Joiner Post is an essential component for creating right angle deviations in your concrete sleeper retaining wall. This product is manufactured by welding two 100 PFC (100mm Parallel Flange Channel) and is suitable for all of our 80mm thick concrete sleepers. Available in lengths to accommodate all of our 200mm high concrete sleepers, this corner joiner is made of hot-rolled steel and hot-dipped galvanised for long-lasting protection against weather, fire and corrosion.

Unlike timber, this product will not fade, rot, warp or be subject to termites, providing you with a safe and durable solution for your retaining wall needs. Furthermore, this corner joiner can be painted to suit the surrounding colour scheme, ensuring that it fits in perfectly with the design of your retaining wall.

With prices starting from $73.35, Retaining Wall Supplies offers a range of 90 Degree Corner Joiner Posts for 80mm Sleepers to suit your retaining wall needs.
